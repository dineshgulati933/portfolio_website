About Me

Hello there, my name is Dinesh Gulati, and I am thrilled to welcome you to my portfolio. I hold a master's degree in Soil and Water Engineering and have experience working with on-field experiments and modeling techniques in the areas related to agricultural and water management.

My academic background has given me a deep understanding of plant-soil-climate relations, and I am passionate about exploring the latest digital advancements in these areas. As someone who considers myself as a "forever student," I am committed to building on my academic foundations in environmental and biological sciences and staying up to date with the latest advancements in the field.

One of my areas of focus is digitization in agriculture, and I have added several skills to my repertoire to further my understanding in this area. I have expertise in mathematical modeling to analyze the relationship between various parameters affecting the soil-water-plant continuum, the use of IoT in agriculture, and programming languages like Python and R for analysis and prediction. I also have experience with remote sensing techniques, such as ArcGIS and Google Earth Engine, for evaluating parameters.

I am excited to share my experiences and skills with you through this portfolio and look forward to discussing any potential opportunities or collaborations. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me!
